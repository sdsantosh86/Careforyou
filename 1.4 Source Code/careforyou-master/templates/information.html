{% trans "Early parenting centers" %}
  • {% trans "Early parenting centers provide a number of different services where their main agenda is to help parents who require additional support to take care of their kids." %}
  • {% trans "They are mainly focused on building ability and talents for parenting, enhancing the bond between parent and child, Strengthening the relation of a family to their culture and Assessing the mental health of children." %}
  • {% trans "Most of these services are accessible for parents who have a kid right from the pregnancy till the child grows to 4 years of age, and as a kid he requires extra help to learn skills and grow up." %}

{% trans "How to choose the best childcare?" %}

    {% trans "When looking for a childcare make sure that the child care provides:" %}

  • {% trans "Provides your kid with valuable playing and learning opportunities." %}
  • {% trans "Ensure that the relationship between employers and the child is loving and consistent." %}

{% trans "How to choose a quality childhood service?" %}
  • {% trans "In a scheme called the National Quality Framework, the quality of child care facilities is rated." %}
  • {% trans "Quality child care and kindergarten are critical, much like colleges, because high-quality early education sets young kids up for life." %}
  • {% trans "The National Quality System is structured to increase the quality of programmes for early childhood. Measuring the consistency of facilities is a major priority of the system." %}
  • {% trans "To help them select early education and treatment, this knowledge is provided to families." %}
  • {% trans "The quality of early childhood services is measured against seven quality areas such as Educational program and practice, Children's health and safety, physical environment, Staffing arrangements, Relationships with children, Collaborative partnerships with families and communities, Leadership and service management." %}

{% trans "Types of childcare" %}
  • {% trans "Long Day care: Open to kids whose families are busy or learning, or who need more time off on a daily basis." %}
  • {% trans "Occasional care: It supports parents with less formal demands, such as the need for an appointment or to relax for two hours." %}
  • {% trans "Family Day Care: Centered in the home of a carer with a very limited number of other kids." %}
  • {% trans "In-Home Care: When in your own house, the caregiver cares for your infant. This is normally referred to as a nanny or au pair." %}

{% trans "How much does childcare cost?" %}
  • {% trans "Many kindergarten curriculum costs are partially charged directly to the kindergarten by the Victorian government. This keeps fees down and allows kids to have a fun experience with their children." %}
  • {% trans "To better cover the expense of running kindergarten classes, all kindergartens charge fees. Specific kindergartens set rates which focus on factors such as how many hours the child attends and if there are added expenses such as excursions." %}
  • {% trans "For their schedule of payments, call the kindergarten you are involved in. To guarantee a child's spot in the curriculum, several kindergartens call for a nominal fee." %}
